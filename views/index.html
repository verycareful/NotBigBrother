The Solution

Double-blind age verification.

We verify your age once. You carry the proof. No one connects the two. Built on Chaum blind signatures — the same math behind anonymous digital cash.

01

You verify once with us

Standard identity check — government ID, bank verification. This is the only moment your identity is involved. We confirm you're 18+, issue a cryptographic token, and immediately discard all identifying information.

02

You hold an anonymous token

Think of it like an anonymous carnival wristband. It proves you passed the age check at the gate, but it has no name on it. Stored locally on your device. Never touches our servers again.

token = Sign(NBB_private_key, {
  type: "age_verified",
  min_age: 18,
  nonce: <random_unique_value>
})
03

You present the token anywhere

No account. No login. No form fields. The website verifies with our public key — locally, offline. We never learn a verification happened.

valid = Verify(NBB_public_key, token)
// That's it. Math checks out. You're in.
